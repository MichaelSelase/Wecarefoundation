About Us

Helping Hands is a nonprofit organization dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and sustainable development programs. Our mission is to create lasting positive change by fostering empowerment and equality.

Our Projects

Community Education

Providing quality education and resources to children and adults to foster skills and literacy in underserved areas.

Healthcare Access

Organizing medical camps and awareness programs to improve health and well-being in remote communities.

Feeding Program

The program aims to reducing hunger,poverty and malnutrition whereby providing free ,nutritionally adequate meals to the public.
